This software package can either generate a metallicity calibration from real or synthetic spectra of RR Lyrae variable stars, or apply to it low-resolution (R ~2000) spectra spanning 3911 to 4950 angstroms.

The calibration builds on that of Layden 1994. See paper [TBD] for details about how the calibration was made, and for further scientific information. If you do use this calibration or software, please cite the paper [TBD] (2023).
