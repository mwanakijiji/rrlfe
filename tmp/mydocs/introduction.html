Introduction¶

This software package applies a metallicity calibration to low-resolution spectra of RR Lyrae variable stars. The calibration is in the style of Layden 94, but with significant updates.

This software is for

  1. Determining and updating a metallicity calibration

  2. Applying a calibration

See paper […] for details about how the calibration was made, and for further scientific information. If you do use this calibration or software, please cite the paper [Authors] (2023).
